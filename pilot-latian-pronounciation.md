Objective: Train the pilot to speak with "Machine Clarity."
The Golden Rule: Spit the Consonants. (Tekan dan letupkan huruf mati).
Focus: The AI struggles with soft endings. You must exaggerate the K, T, and P sounds.

PART 1: The Vocabulary Drill (Latihan Kosakata)

Read these words slowly. Focus on the bold/capitalized letters.

1. COMMAND: DISTRIBUTE (Payload)

Cara Baca: "Dis-TRIB-yut"

Key: The "TR" must be sharp. The final "T" must be clear.

Don't say: "Distribu" (Hilang T) or "Dis-trict".

Practice: "Dis... TRIB... yute."

2. COMMAND: VIRGINIA (Camera)

Cara Baca: "Ver-JIN-ya"

Key: Use a soft "J" sound (like Jakarta), not a hard "G" (like Gudang).

Don't say: "Fir-gi-nia" or "En-gi-neer".

Practice: "Ver... JIN... ya."

3. COMMAND: SUBDURAL (Control/Override)

Cara Baca: "Sab-DYOOR-al"

Key: Emphasize the "SUB". Roll the "R" slightly if needed, but keep "Dural" distinct.

Don't say: "Sub-total" or "Federal".

Practice: "SUB... dural."

4. KEY: SCARLET (Red / Right)

Cara Baca: "SKAR-leT" (Letupan T keras!)

Key: The "K" must be hard. The final "T" is the most important sound.

Don't say: "Skar-le" (Weak T) or "Star-let".

Practice: "Skar... LeT."

5. KEY: COBALT (Blue / Left)

Cara Baca: "KO-bolT" (Letupan T keras!)

Key: Do not say "Kobal". You must hear the "LT" sound at the end.

Don't say: "Go Bold", "Global", "Kabel".

Practice: "Ko... BalT."

PART 3: The "Go Bold" Stress Test (Tes Akses)

This is the specific test to fix the issue where "Go Bold" triggers "Cobalt".

"I will go bold, but the drone is COBALT."

Repeat 5 times:

"Not a Starlet, it is SCARLET."
